About Kate

Relationship coaching for people who want more honesty, steadiness, and self-trust.

Kate’s work is rooted in the belief that healthier relationships begin with a more compassionate and accurate understanding of ourselves.

Book a Discovery Session Contact Kate

Her perspective

Kate helps clients slow down the pattern before it becomes another painful story.

Modern relationships can be complicated: culture, career, distance, attachment, family expectations, and personal history all shape the way people love and protect themselves.

Kate brings a grounded, international perspective to that complexity. Her coaching is warm, direct, and practical. The aim is not to perform confidence, but to build the kind of self-trust that changes how you communicate and choose.

Values

The work is guided by emotional maturity, respect, and practical change.

Truth without shame

Patterns are explored honestly, but without turning your past into a verdict on your future.

Communication with care

Clients practice language for boundaries, needs, repair, and conversations that have been avoided.

Inclusive support

Kate works with clients across cultures, identities, relationship histories, and life stages.
