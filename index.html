About Kate

Kate’s work begins with a simple belief: people do not need more pressure in love. They need more truth, steadiness, and self-understanding.

Her coaching is shaped by the real complexity of modern relationships: ambition, culture, distance, attachment patterns, family expectations, and the quiet fears people carry into connection.

Kate brings warmth and directness to the work. She helps clients slow down enough to hear themselves, then move forward with language, boundaries, and choices that feel more aligned.
