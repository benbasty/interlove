We do not sell personal information. Information is shared only when necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, or protect the integrity of the website and coaching practice.

Your choices and rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information where applicable. You may also unsubscribe from non-essential communication or manage cookies through your browser settings.
