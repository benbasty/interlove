Privacy Policy

We treat personal information with care and use it only to support website functionality, communication, and coaching services.

Effective date: July 16, 2026

Information Use Legal basis Providers Retention Your rights Security Contact

Information we collect

We collect information you choose to provide through inquiry forms, email, quizzes, and coaching-related communication. This can include your name, email address, location, preferred contact time, goals, relationship context, and the content of your message.

When you submit an inquiry form, the submission also includes the page used, referring website origin, campaign source, medium and name when present in the page address, submission time, and browser-reported time zone. These fields help Interlove understand which inquiries are completed and diagnose delivery problems. They do not use cookies or create a cross-site profile.

Please share only information that is reasonably necessary for your inquiry. Website forms are not intended for urgent, medical, or crisis information.

GitHub Pages and third-party services may receive technical information needed to deliver their services, such as IP address, browser and device information, timestamps, referring pages, and security logs.

How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries and coaching requests.
  • To take steps you request before entering a coaching relationship and to provide agreed services.
  • To prepare for sessions and communicate about scheduling or service delivery.
  • To operate, troubleshoot, secure, and improve the website.
  • To understand which website entry points lead to completed inquiries.
  • To meet legal, accounting, safeguarding, and dispute-resolution obligations.

Legal bases for processing

Where data-protection law requires a legal basis, we rely on steps requested by you before a contract and performance of a contract for coaching inquiries and services; legitimate interests in responding to messages, operating a secure website, providing the relationship quizzes, and protecting the practice; and legal obligations where records must be retained or disclosed.

Tally and Typeform load automatically on pages containing their quizzes. You can block third-party content through your browser settings, although doing so may prevent the quizzes from working.

Service providers and international processing

The site is hosted by GitHub Pages. Formspree processes inquiry-form submissions. Tally and Typeform provide quiz embeds that load automatically on their respective pages. Google Fonts supplies typefaces, and the selected email provider processes correspondence.

These providers act under their own terms and privacy notices and may process information in countries different from yours. Where required, providers are expected to use recognized transfer safeguards. Interlove does not sell personal information or share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

How long information is kept

Unsuccessful or preliminary inquiries are ordinarily kept for no longer than 24 months after the last meaningful contact, unless a longer period is needed for a legal claim, safeguarding concern, or another legal obligation. Client, transaction, and accounting records may be retained for the period required by applicable law.

Third-party providers apply their own retention schedules to technical data, cookies, quiz responses, and other information they process.

Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or receive a copy of your information; object to certain processing; withdraw consent; and complain to your local data-protection authority. These rights may be limited by legal exceptions.

Interlove does not use website information to make solely automated decisions with legal or similarly significant effects.

Security

Reasonable organizational and technical measures are used to reduce unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. No website or email transmission is completely secure, so please avoid sending information that is not needed for your inquiry.

Privacy contact and policy changes

Interlove is responsible for the personal information described in this policy. To ask a privacy question or exercise an applicable right, email benbasty@gmail.com. Identity verification may be required before fulfilling a request.

This policy may be updated when services, providers, or legal requirements change. Material revisions will be identified by a new effective date.
