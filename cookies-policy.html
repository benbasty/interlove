Cookie Policy

This page explains how cookies and similar technologies may be used when you visit Interlove, including tools that keep the site working and help us understand what visitors find useful.

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small files stored on your device when you visit a website. They can support basic functionality, remember preferences, and help embedded services load correctly.

Similar technologies, such as pixels or local storage, may also be used by website platforms and third-party tools.

Cookies We May Use

  • Essential cookies: Used for security, navigation, forms, and basic website functionality.
  • Analytics cookies: Used to understand page visits, traffic sources, and aggregate visitor behavior.
  • Embedded-service cookies: Used by forms, scheduling tools, video embeds, fonts, or quiz platforms when those features load.

Third-Party Services

Some cookies may be set by external platforms used on this site, such as form providers, scheduling services, analytics tools, video platforms, or font services. Their cookies are governed by their own privacy and cookie policies.

Interlove does not sell personal information collected through cookies.

Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect embedded forms, booking links, videos, or quiz features.

Most browsers also allow you to clear cookies for a specific website while keeping cookies for other sites.

Updates to This Policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated as the website, services, or third-party tools change. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
