Cookie Policy

This page explains the browser storage and third-party services used by Interlove.

Effective date: July 16, 2026

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small files stored on your device. Similar browser technologies, including local storage, can remember settings or support embedded services. This policy uses “cookies” to describe both cookies and similar storage where appropriate.

Website delivery

Interlove does not currently set its own analytics or advertising cookies. GitHub Pages and security infrastructure may process short-lived technical data needed to deliver and protect the website. Inquiry forms connect to Formspree only when you choose to submit them.

Quiz embeds

Tally loads automatically on the homepage and Typeform loads automatically on the quiz page. These providers may use cookies, local storage, IP addresses, device information, and quiz responses under their own privacy notices.

Interlove does not intentionally deploy advertising or audience-analytics cookies as of the effective date of this policy.

Third-party providers

  • GitHub Pages: website hosting and security.
  • Formspree: inquiry processing when a form is submitted.
  • Tally and Typeform: relationship-quiz embeds that load automatically on their respective pages.
  • Google Fonts: delivery of website typefaces, which can involve a request from your device to Google.

External links to Instagram and YouTube do not load those services until you choose to follow a link. Each provider controls its own cookies and retention practices.

Managing your choices

You can block or clear cookies and site storage through your browser settings. Blocking third-party content may prevent the embedded quizzes from working. Tally and Typeform control cookies or storage they place and provide further choices under their own privacy notices.

Updates to This Policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated when website functionality, providers, or legal requirements change. Material revisions will be identified by a new effective date.
