Cookie Policy

This page explains the browser storage and third-party services used by Interlove, including which optional services require your consent.

Effective date: July 15, 2026

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small files stored on your device. Similar browser technologies, including local storage, can remember settings or support embedded services. This policy uses “cookies” to describe both cookies and similar storage where appropriate.

Essential storage

Interlove stores a privacy-preference value named interlove-consent-v1 in local storage. It records whether you selected essential services only or allowed optional quiz embeds. This is necessary to respect and remember your choice.

GitHub Pages and security infrastructure may process short-lived technical data needed to deliver and protect the website. Inquiry forms connect to Formspree only when you choose to submit them.

Optional quiz embeds

Tally and Typeform are blocked by default. If you select “Allow quiz embeds,” their scripts and embedded content load and those providers may use cookies, local storage, IP addresses, device information, and quiz responses under their own privacy notices.

Interlove does not intentionally deploy advertising or audience-analytics cookies as of the effective date of this policy.

Third-party providers

  • GitHub Pages: website hosting and security.
  • Formspree: inquiry processing when a form is submitted.
  • Tally and Typeform: optional relationship-quiz embeds after consent.
  • Google Fonts: delivery of website typefaces, which can involve a request from your device to Google.

External links to Instagram and YouTube do not load those services until you choose to follow a link. Each provider controls its own cookies and retention practices.

Managing your choices

You can change your Interlove preference at any time. Choosing essential only prevents Tally and Typeform from loading on future page views. It cannot remove cookies a provider placed before consent was withdrawn, so you may also need to clear that provider’s cookies through your browser settings.

Updates to This Policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated when website functionality, providers, or legal requirements change. Material revisions will be identified by a new effective date.
