What to expect.

Is this therapy?

No. Coaching focuses on present patterns, communication, decisions, and relationship skills. It is not mental health treatment.

Can we meet online?

Yes. Online sessions are available for international clients, frequent travelers, and busy professionals.

Will one session be enough?

Some clients need one focused conversation. Others choose ongoing support as they practice new patterns.
