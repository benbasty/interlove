Coaching Scope

Coaching is future-facing and practical. It can help with confidence, communication, dating choices, and relationship patterns, but it is not a substitute for mental health care or trauma treatment.

If therapy, medical support, or specialist care would be more appropriate, Kate will say so clearly and encourage you to work with a licensed professional.

Privacy and Confidentiality

Personal details shared in coaching are treated with care and confidentiality. Interlove does not publish client stories, screenshots, or identifiable details without clear consent.

Getting Started

If you know you want personal support, request a discovery session. If you are still clarifying what you need, start with the Relationship Clarity Quiz.
